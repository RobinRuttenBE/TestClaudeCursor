# O-Parade 2026 — Event Reference

> **Type:** Active campaign / event promotion
> **Status:** Applications open — deadline 3 April 2026
> **Owner:** Denise van Dooren (denise.sempertex@sempertexeurope.com)

---

## Event Summary

Sempertex Europe's annual hands-on balloon build for the O-Parade in Genk, Belgium. 10 selected balloon artists spend 3 days building large-scale toy-themed balloon art and wearable balloon costumes at STX EU HQ, then walk the parade on 1 May wearing the costumes in Genk city centre.

Led by **Denise van Dooren and Audrey Parsons (DnA Events)**. Positioned as "learning first" — experience level doesn't matter, motivation does. This is a masterclass in large-scale event production, not a competition.

## Application Requirements

Applicants send a short motivation letter including:
1. Who they are
2. Their current balloon experience
3. Why they want to be part of this specific team

Selection is based on motivation, not CV or experience level. Beginners welcome.

## Content & Promotion Notes

- Use visuals from previous O-Parade years to set the mood (past builds, costumes, parade shots, crowd reactions)
- Positioning: exclusive, community-driven, learning experience — not just "come build balloons"
- Key selling points: work alongside Denise & Audrey, build at STX EU HQ, walk the parade, be part of something bigger than a workshop
- The €150 price point is intentionally low (food & drinks only) — this is a community/brand event, not a revenue play
- Urgency: only 10 spots, application deadline is firm
