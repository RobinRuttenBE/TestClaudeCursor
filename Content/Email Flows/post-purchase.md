## Email 2: Tips & Tricks

**Timing:** Dag 5
**Doel:** Educatie, productwaarde verhogen, engagement

**Subject Line A:** 3 pro tricks for your new balloons
**Subject Line B:** Get more out of every single balloon
**Preview Text:** Professional techniques that save you time and money.

---

Hi {{first_name}},

Your order should have arrived by now — exciting! Here are three professional techniques to level up your work immediately:

**Trick 1: The Double Stuff**
Take a darker balloon and stuff it inside a lighter one (or vice versa) before inflating. This creates a unique, custom colour effect that your clients will love. Works beautifully with Crystal Clear over any Fashion Solid colour.

**Trick 2: Hi-Float for Longevity**
If you're doing helium installations, Hi-Float is a game changer. Apply it inside the balloon before inflating — your designs will last 10-25x longer. Essential for events where setup is hours before guests arrive.

**Trick 3: Size Matters**
Mixing 5", 12", and 18" balloons in a single organic arrangement creates depth and visual interest that same-size clusters can't match. Start with your largest balloons as anchors, then fill gaps with smaller ones.

Want more? Our YouTube channel has dozens of tutorials — from basic techniques to advanced professional installations.
