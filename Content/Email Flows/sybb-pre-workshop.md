## Email 5: Free Value — Industry Insights

**Timing:** Dag 4
**Doel:** Waarde geven, expertise tonen, reciprociteit opbouwen

**Subject Line A:** 3 pricing rules every new decorator needs to know
**Subject Line B:** How to price your balloon work (without underselling)
**Preview Text:** Most starters charge too little. Here's how to fix that.

---

Hi {{first_name}},

One of the biggest topics we'll cover at the workshop is pricing. But here's a head start — three rules that will save you from the #1 mistake new decorators make:

**Rule 1: Never price by the balloon.**
Your clients aren't buying balloons. They're buying a beautiful space, a wow moment, an experience. Price the outcome, not the materials.

**Rule 2: Know your cost price.**
Before you set any price, you need to know exactly what it costs you — materials, time, transport, setup, teardown. We'll give you a cost price calculator at the workshop to make this easy.

**Rule 3: Your price communicates your positioning.**
Cheap prices attract cheap clients. Premium prices attract clients who value quality. As a Sempertex-trained professional, your work deserves premium pricing.

We'll dive much deeper into this at the workshop, including exact formulas and real-world examples from working decorators.

Consider this your first lesson. The workshop is where it all comes together.

Cheers,
The Sempertex Europe Team
